Beautiful Bermuda website

This website was designed based on the memory of a cruise to the British territory located in the West Atlantic. The website was made to provide information to prospective tourists to the country, such as its culture and history, along with tourist destinations and the country’s efforts to conserve an iconic species, the cahow. Specific fonts were selected to appear friendlier to the audience so that visiting Bermuda would be more attractive. Revisions included adding a “quick facts” section to the home page, and changing the fonts.
