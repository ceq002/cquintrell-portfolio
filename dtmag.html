DigitalTerror Internet Horror Magazine

Case Study: Original design vs. Revisions

This fictional magazine was designed around internet horror, such as the SCP universe, horror podcasts, and the works of Trevor Henderson, along with analog horror such as Local 58, Thalasin, and The Mandela Catalogue. Several different types of magazine pages were included, such as a table of contents and advertisements, along with a feature spread. The fonts were selected based on both horror and the internet.

Software Used: Adobe InDesign

Revisions included:
- The addition of two spreads ("Best YouTube Channels" and "Dr. Nowhere")
- Changes to the cover (font and font color for headline) and table of contents (addition of new spreads and resizing of images)
- Addition of mockup (see above first flipbook)
